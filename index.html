Keiran Suchak

Assorted writings on data and music.

I am a PhD student at the Leeds Institute for Data Analytics, University of Leeds. I have an undergraduate degree in Physics, as well as postgraduate masters degrees in Aerospace Engineering and Mathematics.

My research project focuses on the development of data assimilation methods for the agent-based modelling of pedestrians in urban environments. The project is funded by the ESRC, and is partnered with Leeds City Council.
